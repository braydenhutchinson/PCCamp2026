Future Plans

PSU

The current PSU in my PC, while effective, is white and doesn't match the aesthetic of the rest of the build. This causes both itself and its connected jumble of wires to be noticable even through the ventilation grates at the bottom. Additionally, some cords, such as the 24-pin power adapter, are visible on the motherboard and detract from the overall appearance. I will likely upgrade to a Corsair CX650, as it can not only be the right color for the PC, but also provides more power to support a better GPU.

RAM

The current RAM in my PC is sufficient for most tasks, but I may consider upgrading to higher capacity, faster speed, or even just more aesthetically pleasing RAM in the future. Obviously, my performance will also require more sticks of RAM to hopefully eventually fill the remaining available slots. I will likely use either the Teamgroup T-Force Delta or the Teamgroup T-Force Extreem for their good performance, reasonable pricing, and RGB lighting.

Storage

My current SSD card contains 120 GB of storage space, which is enough for most reasonable activity on the computer. Many people end up upgrading to larger capacity drives in the future, a very popular upgrade being the common 2 TB SSD, such as the relatively affordable Silicon Power UD90. This is likely what I will do as well.

GPU

My current GPU is a very budget, low-quality card that doesn't perform well under heavy loads. I do not plan to be using any graphics-intensive applications in the near future, however I will still need to upgrade it enough to run mid to high-end games. My current plan is to use a GeForce RTX 4060, as it is relatively cheap and performs better than many other GPUs.
